Privacy Policy — CloudCert: AWS Exam Prep

Last updated: July 2026

CloudCert: AWS Exam Prep ("the App") is developed and maintained by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information.

Summary

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection, an account, or a login of any kind.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • The App does not request the Internet permission and cannot send data anywhere.
  • The App does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • The App does not require sign-in, and does not collect names, emails, or device identifiers.
  • The App does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your quiz history, progress, and spaced-repetition study data locally on your device only, using a local database. This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to the developer, and is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App. You can also clear it at any time from within the App (Progress Dashboard > Clear History).

Contact / Feedback

The App includes an optional "Send Feedback" button that opens your email app to compose a message to the developer. This is entirely user-initiated — the App does not send anything automatically. If you choose to email the developer, that message is subject to your own email provider's privacy practices, not this policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it collects no information at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future (for example, if optional analytics or online features are introduced), this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.
